Artist Statement



My name is Mona.
Studying Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. I used to make pencil/charcoal drawing most, but I’m interested in more digital art such as programming, shooting the video, making a game etc.
My goal as an artist is to make an art pieces related to the life. Since I feel the beauty to the things which is changing, I want audience to feel what the meaning of life is to them.
