ARTIST BIO


Mona Kaji is an artist who was born and raised in Japan. She has surrounded herself by art and has been interested in fabrication since she was a child. She challenged herself in making drawings, paintings, and digital art such as video editing, coding, or modeling. Her inspiration is based on her culture and she is trying to combine it with art today. She has a passion for building better mental health and education.
