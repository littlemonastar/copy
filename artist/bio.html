ARTIST BIO


Mona Kaji is an artist who was born and raised in Japan. She has surrounded herself by art and has been interested in fabrication since she was a child. She challenged herself in making drawings, paintings, and digital art such as video editing, coding, or modeling. She is passionate about creating content related to education and mental health. She works as an artist and entrepreneur.
