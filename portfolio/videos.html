VIDEOS

These videos are from the project in ART 75.


1. #Wasted / 2018 / Short Movie



It is all about social media addiction. I use Instagram as everyone does, but am sometimes worried about people who only cares the photos. They want to take a photo of food with good quality, but don't care its taste. They want to take self portrait like model, but don't care people around them even though they bother. People are too much on their phone while they are sitting together at restaurant.


2. The Difference / 2019 / Stop Motion Animation



This is a simple message that we should accept the difference. When we face a situation that we have never experienced before, we tend to avoid seeing the truth or accepting something new like not only appearance but also the behavior or culture. Before we judge and act, we should consider how others see their world and feel what you would do.


3. A Map to Get Lost / 2018



This project is from ART 2 and I created my own map inspired by a quote from Yoko Ono "A map to get lost". Life is a series of choices. We seem to make an average of 70 choices a day. The choice can be wrong or correct. I think that people might get lost when they are looking at the graph of a lifetime and wonder if what was correct. This is what this video shows.
