ARTIST STATEMENT


Mona Kaji is an artist who was born and raised in Japan. Moved to the US in 2012 and studied Mathematics and Art at Foothill College. She is currently studying Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Has worked as a web designer after graduating from Foothill college.

She makes drawings, paintings, and does video editing and coding. She likes drawing influenced by surrealism or Zen art. Her recent work is inspired by the games she used to play in childhood and she often makes pixel art. As part of her extracurricular activities, she is involved in developing apps for acquaintances.
